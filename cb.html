Computer Application&Business Management

computer Application And Business Management

Government Residential Women’s Polytechnic College, Payyannur is one among the five government polytechnics offering the course CABM, with an intake of 52 students. The course is entitled to impart both theoretical and practical knowledge of modern business to the students. This course provides insight to theories and practices on business and inter-disciplinary areas of management and applications of computers in business. Students from the department are able to handle computerized operations of all business activities, proficient in office automation necessary for business management, dynamic enough for handling and assisting all business operations including marketing, sales & customer care and well trained to assist office administration. Candidates for admission shall be required to have passed Xth class examination (S.S.L.C.) conducted by the Board of Public Examinations, Kerala State or any other equivalent examinations already recognized by the Board of Public Examinations, of Kerala State, with the eligibility for Higher Education.There is a full-fledged computer lab and competent faculty to assist and guide the students. After completing Polytechnic diploma in Computer Application & Business Management, a student can apply for B. Tech in Computer Science & Engineering / Information Technology through lateral entry scheme. Students can also apply for private sector jobs as well as government sector jobs, as: o Office Assistant o Project Assistant o Office Administrator o Admin Executive o Admin Assistant o Personnel Assistant and Executive Assistant o Asst. Graphic Designer o Data Entry Operator and more.
