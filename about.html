About Us

Our Journey Of History-

"We Are The Only Govt.Polytechnic College For Women In The State Of Kerala With A "Residential" Status"

Government Residential Women's Polytechnic College ,Payyanur wa established under World Bank Assisted Technician Education Project (WBATEP) in 1991.It is a Government Polytechnic under Directorate of technical Education.Electronics & Communication,Instrumentation Engineering,Computer Engineering and Computer Application & Business Management are the four emerging Diploma level programmes offered in this Institution.

This institute has been catering to the industry by providing high quality technician manpower and offering sustainable development to the nearby villages through its initiatives like Community Development Through Polytechnic Scheme sponsored by the Skill Development Ministry Government of India.
